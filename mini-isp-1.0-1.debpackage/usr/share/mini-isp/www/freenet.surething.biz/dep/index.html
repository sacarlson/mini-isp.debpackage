FreeNET Voucher Instructions


To make use of your FreeNet Vouchers there are two methods of use:


1. deposit the money in your FreeNet account by going to: http://freenet.surething.biz/deposit.html You will be asked to enter the Voucher code number into the text box and then press submit botton. It will then divert to the FreeNet login page. If you don't have an account yet then you will have to select the create new account link. If you have an account then enter your login email and password and submit. If the voucher code is valid, The value will be added to your voucher credit in your account. With the voucher credit you can buy any service in the FreeNet store. At checkout of your purchase in the store select use voucher credit to complete your transaction. The purchase will deduct the cost from your FreeNet voucher balance.


2. Option two if you have an account at FreeNet you can pick the service you want and checkout when your finished. At the end of checkout there is an option to enter the voucher number. You can enter the voucher number and select voucher as payment method. The cost of your purchase will come from the voucher value.
