What is Freenet WiFi

Freenet is funded and operated by individuals and volunteers - in one sense, like public television. Freenet was created by a group of people who felt there was a need to have high speed access to the Internet in all the places they might use them. At the time there were already WiFi points near by but none were at a price that seemed fair. The group now named The Freenet Group found that as a group they could share access to one or many access points at a fraction of the cost of what it then cost to connect to other systems. As a group they found they could pool resources and money to make a better and faster system. Freenet is a nonprofit organization. The money that is received goes to continued operation, system maintenance and in making improvements in the system. Improvements include extending the range of operation and adding higher speed Internet access from our providers. The growth and continued existence relies on your donations. So please donotate what you feel the service is worth to you. Remember you are not only helping yourself but everyone around you that can also make use of it.

We are also looking for more locations to install more access points. If you have access to a location that already has a phone line or better yet ADSL service you are willing to share, please contact us. We (our staff) will study the locations provided and decide the best locations for expansion that we feel will provide the best benefit to the community.
